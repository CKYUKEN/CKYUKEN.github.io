About us

Our Objectives

Vision:

To be one of the main organizations in Hong Kong for the university students to manage their stress from different ways.

Mission:

Facing different sources of stress is common for the students in Hong Kong.

We would like to provide different kind of choices and facilities for the students to release their stress.

What make us difference?

Absolute Private Areas

We believe that for the stress people, they desired a purely personal space for the them to do whatever they wanted. Sometimes, they may only want to do nothing, but simply stay alone. We would then like to provide such private areas for them to release their stress efficiently.

Flexible Booking Systems

We understand that the university students in Hong Kong are all busy and they may got stress any time, any where. We may then would like to provide several ways for them to make a booking to enjoy our service. To release their stress as soon as possible.

Various Room Choices

We find that for different peopel, they may have different way to release their stress. Some of them may eat a lots; some of them may do something silly; some of them may sing a song. We understand that there is no fixed formula for the students to release their stress. So We provide different choices for the students.
